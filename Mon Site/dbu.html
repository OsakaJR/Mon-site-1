Dragon Ball Universe

Dragon Ball

Dragon Ball is a Japanese media franchise that follows the adventures of Goku, a powerful warrior who protects the Earth from various villains and threats. Alongside a group of allies, he searches for the mystical Dragon Balls while facing numerous challenges and battles. The story spans several series and arcs, featuring martial arts techniques, transformations, and epic battles against powerful enemies. Despite its many iterations, Dragon Ball has remained a beloved franchise for over three decades, inspiring numerous spin-off series, movies, and video games.


Dragon Ball Z

Dragon Ball Z continues the story of Goku and his friends as they face more powerful enemies and threats to Earth, including the Saiyans, Frieza, and the androids. Along the way, they gain new allies and powers and engage in epic battles, including Goku's iconic transformation into a Super Saiyan. The series concludes with the defeat of Majin Buu and Goku leaving Earth to train a new generation of warriors. Dragon Ball Z has had a massive cultural impact, inspiring numerous video games, movies, and merchandise.


Dragon Ball GT

Dragon Ball GT follows Goku, transformed into a child, as he travels across the universe with Pan and Trunks to collect the Black Star Dragon Balls and return to his normal form. Along the way, they face off against various villains and discover new transformations, including Super Saiyan 4. Although criticized for its departure from the original story and character development, Dragon Ball GT remains popular among fans for its epic battles and iconic moments.


Dragon Ball Super

Dragon Ball Super continues the story of Goku and his friends as they face powerful foes, including gods of destruction and other universes in a multiverse tournament. Along the way, they gain new transformations and abilities, such as Super Saiyan Blue and Ultra Instinct. The series has been praised for its improved animation and storytelling, and has introduced new characters that have had a significant impact on the Dragon Ball franchise.
